In this paper we present the SimCardioTest cloud-based platform, which offers healthcare companies and researchers integrated and user-friendly simulation workflows for in silico testing during the development and validation of new cardiac medical devices and drugs. Based on a web-based collaboration paradigm, the platform represents an innovative solution for the life sciences and healthcare environments, ultimately resulting in an increased pace of innovation and a reduced time-to-market for new treatments.
